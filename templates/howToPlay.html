BLACKJACK

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand.
The player starts with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end.
To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn.

If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand.
If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & 10), you got a blackjack and instantly win.

Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 17 or higher.

SUDOKU

The goal of sudoku is to fill the 9x9 grid with each column and row having numbers from 1 - 9.

If a number repeats in a row, column, or box, it will turn red because that is invalid.
If a number is not a number from 1 - 9, ie. 0 or # or fosn, it will also turn red.

Lastly, when you think you are complete (all boxes are filled and none are red), then press the 'Finish' button to get your coins.

DICE ROLLER

Roll the dice.
The sum of the first two dice have to be greater than the sum of the last two dice to win.
